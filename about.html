About Magic City Savers

What We're Building

Magic City Savers is a hyper-local grocery savings club built for Birmingham neighborhoods. Our goal is simple: make it easy for shoppers to compare prices across nearby stores — like Aldi, Publix, Walmart, Piggly Wiggly, and more — so everyone can save money every week.

We're building tools that scrape and analyze grocery prices across multiple stores, letting you build a shopping list and see which store has the best deals in real time.

Why This Matters

Grocery prices have climbed year after year, and shoppers often have no visibility into which store is truly cheapest for their weekly list. Many families in our area are spending far more than they need to — simply because the information is hard to find.

By gathering price data, listening to local shoppers, and focusing on a few neighborhoods at a time, we aim to create a tool that delivers real savings, week after week.
